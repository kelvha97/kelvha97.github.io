Who I am

Recent Work

Fountana di Trevi

The Trevi Fountain, built on an ancient aquaduct, showcases one of the largest and oldest water sources in Rome.

Market at Campo di Fiori

This old square, right next to the UW Rome Center is famous for its bustling markets, shops and nightlife. The name Campo di Fiori translates to "field of flowers" where it use to be exactly that.

Lalaria Beach

Located in Skiathos, Greece, this beach presented itself with the magazine touch, with the backdrop of the sunny skies, its clear-blue waters and the formation of the natural arch, it all came together to represent the hidden beauty of our Earth.

Ponte Garibaldi

The daily bridge I use to walk on to go from my apartment to the UW Rome Center. It connects the center of Rome to the nearing neighborhood of Trastevere where I lived.

The Old Port of Skiathos, Greece

The island of Skiathos with its long past history of being a small fishing village, now sits and welcomes new travelers to experience its natural beauty in all its glory.

The Streets of Rome

The narrow streets, the cobblestone roads and the abundance of locals walking sums up my experience in Rome as a student traveler. An incredible experience in a new country with a different environment, culture and history.
